Soekarno

The First President of Indonesia and Proclamator of Independence

The Life of a Revolutionary Leader

  • 1901 - Born in Surabaya, East Java
  • 1928 - Founded the Indonesian National Party (PNI)
  • 1945 - Proclaimed Indonesia's independence alongside Mohammad Hatta
  • 1945-1967 - Served as the first President of Indonesia
  • 1955 - Organized the Asian-African Conference in Bandung
  • 1970 - Passed away in Jakarta, Indonesia

"Never forget history, because from history we can learn to build a better future."

- Soekarno
